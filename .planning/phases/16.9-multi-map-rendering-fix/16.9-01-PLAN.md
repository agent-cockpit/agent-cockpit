Purpose: Foundation for multi-map rendering — both the visual layer (TilemapRenderer) and the physics layer (CollisionMap) must understand multiple maps before OfficePage can be wired.
Output: Updated TilemapRenderer.ts and CollisionMap.ts with new test coverage; existing tests remain green.
</objective>

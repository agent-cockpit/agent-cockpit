## Decisions Made

- `tick: number` made required rather than optional in `DrawAgentSpriteOptions` — optional would allow callers to omit it and silently get NaN for col, producing blank frames. Required forces immediate TypeScript error at all call sites.
- `NPC_FRAME_COUNTS` inlined in function body alongside `NPC_TICKS_PER_FRAME` rather than as module-level exports — co-located with usage, avoids unnecessary export surface.

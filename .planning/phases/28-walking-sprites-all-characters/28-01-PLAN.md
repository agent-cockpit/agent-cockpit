<objective>
Generate 8-frame × 8-direction walk cycles for 4 characters — robot, alien, hologram, monkey — via the PixelLab v2 REST API (`animate_character` endpoint with `walking-8-frames` template), and save the decoded RGBA frames as PNG files in `assets/raw/{character}/walk/{direction}/frame-N.png`.

Purpose: These are the raw frames that `scripts/build-spritesheet.ts` will assemble into walk rows of each character's sprite sheet in Phase 28-03. Without these PNG files, characters other than astronaut will keep falling back to the fight-stance "blocked" animation when walking.

Output: 4 × 8 directions × 8 frames = 256 PNG frames on disk across 32 direction subdirectories.
</objective>

        .png()
        .toFile(path.join(dir, `frame-${i}.png`))

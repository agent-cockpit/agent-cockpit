  <action>
    Process characters ONE AT A TIME in this order: robot, alien, hologram, monkey, caveman.
    Do NOT queue multiple characters simultaneously — PixelLab rate limit is ~3-5 concurrent jobs.

    For EACH character, follow this exact sequence:

    1. Call create_character with the character's description and the shared parameters above.
    2. Poll get_character(character_id) every 10 seconds until status != 'processing'.
       If status='failed': retry once with ai_freedom:400 before moving on.
    3. Call animate_character for 'idle' (breathing-idle template, all 8 directions). Poll until done.
    4. Call animate_character for 'blocked' (fight-stance-idle-8-frames, all 8 directions). Poll until done.
    5. Call animate_character for 'completed' (jumping-1, all 8 directions). Poll until done.
    6. Call animate_character for 'failed' (falling-back-death, all 8 directions). Poll until done.
    7. Download all frame PNGs for all 4 states × 8 directions to:
       assets/raw/{character}/{state}/{direction}/frame-N.png
    8. Run: npx tsx scripts/build-spritesheet.ts {character}
       This writes packages/ui/public/sprites/{character}-sheet.png + {character}-manifest.json
    9. Append row to scripts/generation-log.md:
       | {character} | {character_id} | done | {idle_frames}/dir | {blocked_frames}/dir | {completed_frames}/dir | {failed_frames}/dir | done |

    Style consistency check: After generating each character's base, compare to Astronaut.
    If the art style looks dramatically different (different outline weight, pixel scale), retry with these anchors appended to description: ", FTL crew member art style, consistent black outline, low detail"
    and ai_freedom reduced to 400.

    IMPORTANT: Complete all 5 characters in this task before ending. Use polling with exponential backoff:
    - First 3 polls: every 10 seconds
    - After 3 polls: every 30 seconds
    - Timeout after 10 minutes per character (log and move on if exceeded)
  </action>

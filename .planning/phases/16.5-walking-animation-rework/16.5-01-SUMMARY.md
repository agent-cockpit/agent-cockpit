## Accomplishments

- All 40 PlayerInput tests pass with 0 failures

- `packages/ui/src/game/__tests__/PlayerInput.test.ts` — 4 new regression tests appended to `movePlayer — animTime` block

## Decisions Made

- `WALK_FRAME_DURATION_MS = 100`: At 150ms/frame, 8 frames takes 1200ms — the sprite travels too far between foot-plants making it look like a slide. 100ms/frame (10fps, 800ms cycle) matches a natural humanoid walk pace.
- `isMoving = dx !== 0 || dy !== 0`: The old guard `up || down || left || right` fires animTime accumulation even when opposing keys cancel net movement (dx=0, dy=0). Using displacement ensures the animation only advances when the character actually moves.

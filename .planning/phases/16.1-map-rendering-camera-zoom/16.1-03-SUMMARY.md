- Click-to-teleport hit-test fixed to divide screen coords by zoom before adding camera offset

- map-composite.png (3232x3232) copied to packages/ui/public/map/ and served correctly
- Human visual verification passed — tilemap visible in browser, camera follow working, NPC click accurate

## Decisions Made

- Used map-composite.png fallback instead of Wang tileset PNG reconstruction — only 2 of 7 tileset PNGs existed in the Cockpit Map-export directory; loading the pre-rendered composite is simpler, correct, and avoids missing asset errors
- TilemapRenderer.load() remains fire-and-forget (engine starts immediately, map appears once ready) — no blocking on asset load

## Deviations from Plan

### Auto-fixed Issues

**1. [Rule 1 - Bug] Simplified TilemapRenderer to use map-composite.png instead of missing tileset PNGs**
- **Found during:** Task 1 / Task 2 checkpoint (visual verification)
- **Issue:** TilemapRenderer was designed to reconstruct the map from individual Wang tileset PNGs, but only 2 of 7 expected tileset PNGs were present in the Cockpit Map-export directory. This caused 5 failed image loads and a largely blank OffscreenCanvas.
- **Fix:** Copied `Cockpit Map-export/map-composite.png` (pre-rendered 3232x3232 composite) to `packages/ui/public/map/map-composite.png`. Rewrote TilemapRenderer.ts to load this single image and blit it directly — zero missing assets, full map visible.
- **Files modified:** `packages/ui/src/game/TilemapRenderer.ts`, `packages/ui/public/map/map-composite.png`
- **Verification:** Map visible in browser; all 67 unit tests still pass
- **Committed in:** `9ccd70f`

## Decisions Made

- **South-only animation strategy:** Generate animations for south direction only initially. Reduces cost from 8 × 4 = 32 gens to 8 × 1 = 8 gens. The current AgentSprite implementation shows one facing direction regardless, so all-direction animation provides no immediate UI value.
- **Chibi proportions:** At 32px, a large head ratio is essential for readability. Chibi is the standard top-down RPG character proportion and ensures the glowing visor / key facial features are visible.
- **Templates always over custom:** Template mode costs 1 gen/direction. Custom (v3) mode costs 20–40 gens/direction. All 8 AgentAnimState values have a viable template mapping.

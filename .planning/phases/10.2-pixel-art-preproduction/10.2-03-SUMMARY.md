## Decisions Made

- **South-only animation is the clear recommendation:** 8 gens for all states vs 32 gens (4-dir) or 64 gens (8-dir). Current `AgentSprite` renders single-direction only. Reserve directional capability by creating with `n_directions:4` but animating `directions:["south"]` only.
- **Prototype-first ordering:** Generate 1–3 anchor character prototypes before committing to any animations. Lock `character_id` before queueing 8 animation jobs. This prevents wasting animation budget on a rejected character design.
- **Budget risk is effectively zero:** 64 planned images vs 2000 available. Even 20× retry rate on every single asset keeps total under 1280 (64% of budget). The tier allocation caps are organizational discipline, not budget protection.
- **Icon generation via `create_map_object`:** All UI icons (status dots, risk badges, action icons, tab icons) use `create_map_object` at 1 gen each — 34 icons total in Tier 2. Alternative was manual pixel editing; PixelLab generation is faster even at 1 attempt per icon.
